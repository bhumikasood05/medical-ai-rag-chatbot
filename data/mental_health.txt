Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

Common mental health conditions include:
- Anxiety disorders
- Depression
- Stress-related disorders

Symptoms may include:
- Persistent sadness
- Excessive worry
- Mood changes
- Difficulty concentrating
- Sleep disturbances
- Loss of interest in activities

Maintaining mental health involves:
- Seeking support from family and friends
- Practicing stress-management techniques
- Exercising regularly
- Consulting mental health professionals when needed

Early intervention and treatment can improve outcomes.

This information is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.
