Good nutrition is essential for maintaining overall health and preventing diseases.

A balanced diet should include:
- Fruits and vegetables
- Whole grains
- Lean proteins
- Healthy fats
- Dairy or calcium-rich foods

Healthy eating habits include:
- Drinking enough water
- Limiting processed foods
- Reducing excess sugar intake
- Avoiding excessive salt consumption

Proper nutrition supports growth, immunity, and energy levels.

Consult a nutrition expert for personalized dietary advice.
