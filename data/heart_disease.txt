Heart disease refers to various conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels.

Common symptoms include:
- Chest pain
- Shortness of breath
- Fatigue
- Dizziness
- Irregular heartbeat

Risk factors include:
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Obesity
- Lack of physical activity
- Family history

Prevention includes a healthy diet, regular exercise, avoiding smoking, and routine health check-ups.

Seek immediate medical attention if severe chest pain occurs.
