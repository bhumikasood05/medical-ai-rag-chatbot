The immune system protects the body against infections and diseases.

Ways to strengthen immunity include:
- Eating a balanced diet
- Getting enough sleep
- Exercising regularly
- Managing stress
- Staying hydrated
- Maintaining good hygiene

Vaccinations help the immune system recognize and fight harmful organisms.

A weakened immune system may increase the risk of infections.

Consult a healthcare professional for concerns related to immune health.
