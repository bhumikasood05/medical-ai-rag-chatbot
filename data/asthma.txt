Asthma is a chronic condition that causes inflammation and narrowing of the airways in the lungs.

Common symptoms include:
- Wheezing
- Shortness of breath
- Chest tightness
- Persistent coughing, especially at night

Common triggers include:
- Dust mites
- Pollen
- Smoke
- Pet dander
- Cold air
- Respiratory infections
- Physical exercise

Asthma can be managed using inhalers, prescribed medications, and by avoiding triggers.

Regular follow-up with a healthcare professional is important.
